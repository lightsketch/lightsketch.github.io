Intro

I 'm a 2D game developer and web designer. Working on games whenever there's free time, including making pixel art, sprites, animations and music.
Skills: Concept design and illustration of a variety of subject matters in different styles, both digitally and traditionally. Good at verbal and written communication. Low poly 3D modeling and texturing.
Proficient in use of Photoshop, Clip Studio Paint, Sketch, Aseprite, 3D Max.

Objective: To work on fun and challenging projects in a team-oriented environment. To grow artistically and share my passion and knowledge with co-workers.

About

I use a lot of different programs and tools in my workflow

Design:

With years of profesional expirience in Photoshop I have made and exported files for websites and games. Edited photos and created enviroments from scratch. Illustrator and Sketch are perefect for creating vector images and website mockups, as well as some game and app files and layouts. I prefer Sketch due it's ease of use and many plugins.

Clip Studio is my softwear of choice when it comes to making comics and coloring and character concepts. And Asperite is one of my favorite applications where I can do almost anything in pixel art - create sceens and animations ready for ingame use.

Programing:

Expirience with HTML5 and CSS3 as well as the basics of Java Scrip and PHP. Years of expirience using Git to develop sites and games with other memebers. 4 + Expirience with Linux and macOS. I can get my way around in the terminal as well as make a couple of Bash scripts.

3D:

3D Max is my choice for low poly objects and most indoor scenes. While Zbrush is best for organinc and high poly chracters (still learning).

Music:

Ableton Live and Audacity are the programs I use to create background music and instrumentals for games.
